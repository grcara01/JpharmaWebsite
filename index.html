Atorvastatin Calcium

Manufacturer: Pfizer Inc.

Atorvastatin is used along with a proper diet to help lower "bad" cholesterol and fats (such as LDL, triglycerides) and raise "good" cholesterol (HDL) in the blood. It belongs to a group of drugs known as "statins." It works by reducing the amount of cholesterol made by the liver.

This product is available in 10mg, 20mg, 40mg, and 80mg tablets. Minimum order quantity applies.

Insulin Glargine

Manufacturer: Sanofi

Insulin glargine is a long-acting insulin used to control high blood sugar in adults and children with diabetes mellitus. It starts working several hours after injection and keeps working evenly for 24 hours.

Available in 10mL vials (100 units/mL) and 3mL prefilled pens. Requires prescription and proper storage conditions.

Patient Monitor

Advanced patient monitoring system with 12.1" high-resolution touchscreen display. Monitors ECG, SpO2, NIBP, temperature, and more. Features include:

  • Trend analysis
  • Network connectivity

Ideal for hospitals, clinics, and critical care units.

Amoxicillin Capsules

Manufacturer: Sandoz

Amoxicillin is a penicillin antibiotic that fights bacteria. It is used to treat many different types of infection caused by bacteria, such as tonsillitis, bronchitis, pneumonia, and infections of the ear, nose, throat, skin, or urinary tract.

Available in 250mg and 500mg capsules. Pack sizes of 100, 500, and 1000 capsules. Requires prescription.
